Seed Generator
-----------------------------------------------------------
Generates a 2D array of ints 0-X
     X being the number of differnt tile types
-----------------------------------------------------------
Tiles types
-----------------------------------------------------------
0 - plains, open land
1 - forest
2 - mountain
3 - river
4 - lake
5 - pond
6 - forested hills
7 - open hills
7 - desert
8 - valley
9 - canyon
10 - badlands
11 - lava land
12 - lava lake
13 - lava ocean
14 - swamp
15 - marsh
16 - jungle
17 - jungle hills
18 - ocean
19 - beach
20 - ice cap
